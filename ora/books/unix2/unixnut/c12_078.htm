Chapter 12
nroff and troff
 

.ti

.ti [[±]n]

Temporary indent. Indent the next output line by n or increment the current indent by ±n for the next output line. Default scale is ems.

Example

.in 10
.ti -5
The first line of this paragraph sticks out by 5 ems ...
.in -10
