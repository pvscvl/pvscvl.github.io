Quotations

The Lewis Carroll quotations that begin each chapter are from the Millennium Fulcrum Edition 2.9 of the Project Gutenberg electronic text of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland and Through the Looking-Glass. Quotations in Chapters 1, 2, 5, 6, 8, and 13 come from Alice's Adventures in Wonderland, and those in Chapters 3, 4, 7, 9, 11, 12, and 15 come from Through the Looking-Glass.
