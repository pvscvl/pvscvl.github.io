9.7. Configuring the Canvas Widget

As usual, to configure or get information about the Canvas widget, you can use the configure and cget methods, explained in detail in Chapter 13, "Miscellaneous Perl/Tk Methods". Remember, configure and cget operate on the entire Canvas widget (possibly affecting the items within it).
