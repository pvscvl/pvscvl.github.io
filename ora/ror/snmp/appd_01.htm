This appendix provides a brief list of all the SNMP RFCs, along with the status of each RFC. This list (often referred to as the Standards Summary) was taken from The Simple Times, an online publication that should be familiar to anyone working with SNMP. It is used with their permission and can be found in each quarterly edition of the magazine. Please go to http://www.simple-times.org for information on how to subscribe to this free publication.
